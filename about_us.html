About Us

Selva Plantas is a family-owned online plant store based in Lisbon, Portugal. We are passionate about plants and committed to offering the highest quality products and services to our customers. With a wide range of plants and gardening products, we aim to make the world a greener place, one plant at a time.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with the best selection of plants and gardening products, expert advice, and exceptional customer service. We believe that by nurturing plants, we also nurture our souls and contribute to a healthier planet.

Our Team

Our dedicated team of plant enthusiasts works tirelessly to ensure that we offer the finest plants and products to our customers. With years of experience in the horticulture industry, our team is committed to helping you find the perfect plant for your space.

Get In Touch

We'd love to hear from you! If you have any questions, suggestions, or feedback, please don't hesitate to reach out to us through our Contact Us page.
